# Liora - A Guided Space for Meaningful Digital Presence

Liora is a creative studio focused on crafting digital experiences that feel warm, intentional, and human.  
This website serves as an interactive introduction to who we are, what we create, and how we collaborate.

The experience is designed to feel **alive** using thoughtful motion, soft gradients, slow reveal transitions, and intentional spacing. The goal is not just to show what Liora does, but to express how we think.

---

## Core Sections

### **Welcome to Liora**

A soft landing space highlighting the essence of Liora and our approach to design and brand building.

### **Our Journey**

A storytelling section about growth, evolution, and the philosophy that guides our work.

### **What We Create**

A curated look at our capabilities, services, and the values that shape every client partnership.

### **Get in Touch**

A simple and honest contact interface - built to invite conversation rather than transaction.

---

## Tech Stack

- **React** + **Vite** (or Create React App depending on build context)
- **React Router** for page transitions
- **Framer Motion** for animation and interactive movement
- **TailwindCSS** for styling and system-level visual consistency

---

## Design Principles

- Calm motion over flashy animation
- Spacious layouts with balanced rhythm
- Color tones inspired by warmth and natural light
- Interfaces that **invite curiosity rather than demand attention**
